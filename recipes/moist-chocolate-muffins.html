Moist chocolate muffins

Description

Very moist, tasty and full of chocolate goodness! Even better the next day.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • ¾ cup chocolate chips/li>
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • ¼ cup chocolate chips
